About Us

Black Flame Group is an unofficial non-profit organization founded in 2024 by Ziad AL-Nahlawi, a 22-year-old Computer Science student at Manara University. Our goal is simple yet ambitious: to make the world a better place.

While we are currently working on becoming an official organization, our commitment to positive change remains unwavering. We believe that through dedication, innovation, and collaboration, we can create meaningful impact in our communities and beyond.

Join us in our journey to ignite change and illuminate the path towards a brighter future for all.
